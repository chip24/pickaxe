Martika, born Marta Marrero, was a prominent pop singer and actress in the late 1980s and early 1990s.
Her biggest hit, "Toy Soldiers," reached number one on the U.S. charts, and she sold over four million albums worldwide.
Martika also had success on television, appearing in shows like "Kids Incorporated" before stepping away from the spotlight due to burnout in the early 1990s.
